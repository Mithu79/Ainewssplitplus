Here is your **Moltbot YouTube Automation Bot** — a fully AI-powered dashboard built for Indian and Asian content creators.

---

**What's Inside (4 Core Modules)**

**🎬 Video Prompts Tab** — Enter any topic, choose a style (Cinematic, Documentary, Tutorial, etc.) and your AI video tool (Sora, Flow, Runway, Kling, Pika, etc.). Claude generates a complete scene-by-scene breakdown with optimized prompts for that specific tool, plus a ready-to-use YouTube title and description.

**🖼️ Thumbnail Tab** — Enter your video title, niche, and mood (Shocking, Inspiring, Dramatic, etc.), and Claude generates 3 high-CTR thumbnail prompt variations with text overlay suggestions, color schemes, and AI image generation prompts ready for Midjourney or DALL-E.

**📅 Scheduler Tab** — Build a full weekly upload calendar with day, time, and topic slots. The AI Schedule Optimizer analyzes your plan and suggests the best upload times specifically for Indian and Asian audiences.

**🚀 Auto-Upload Tab** — Select your platform (YouTube, Shorts, Instagram Reels, TikTok) and automation stack (n8n, Make.com, Zapier, etc.), and Claude generates a complete step-by-step automation pipeline guide — no coding required.

---

**How to Use It**

Open any tab, fill in the fields, and click the generate button. All AI outputs include a one-click **Copy** button so prompts can be pasted directly into Sora, Flow, Midjourney, or your automation tool of choice.
